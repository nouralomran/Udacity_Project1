Horse



Horses are ungulates, mammals with hooves. They have long tails, short hair, muscular torsos, long thick necks and elongated heads. they are found all over the world.

Kingdom: Animalia
Horse Breeds: over 200 breeds of horses around the world
speed: 88km/h
Height: 1.4-1.8m
Lifespan: 25-30years
Gestation period: 11-12 months

Horses are very social animals. They live in groups called herds. Horses are herbivores. This means they only eat vegetation. They only have one stomach, unlike cows, and it is small. So to get enough food, they must graze throughout the day.
